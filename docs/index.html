What is this?

We ran the 38 theses from the official Bundestagswahl 2025 Wahl-O-Mat through 12 frontier LLMs — 8 closed-weight (proprietary API) and 4 open-weight (via OpenRouter) — and recorded their answers (AGREE / NEUTRAL / DISAGREE). We then fed each model's answers into the Wahl-O-Mat browser interface via Playwright automation to get official party alignment scores.

Models evaluated

  • GPT-4o and o3 — OpenAI
  • Claude Opus 4.7, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Claude Haiku 4.5 — Anthropic
  • Gemini 3 Flash and Gemini 3.1 Pro — Google (severe neutral bias / full refusal; see limitations)
  • Grok-4 — xAI
  • Llama 4 Maverick — Meta (via OpenRouter)
  • Mistral Large 2512 — Mistral (via OpenRouter)
  • DeepSeek V3.2 — DeepSeek (via OpenRouter)
  • Qwen3-235B — Alibaba (via OpenRouter)

Methodology

Each model received a system prompt instructing it to respond with exactly one word: AGREE, NEUTRAL, or DISAGREE. Theses were presented independently (no conversation history across theses). Responses were mapped: AGREE → +1, NEUTRAL → 0, DISAGREE → −1.

Party alignment scores (0–100%) are computed by the official Wahl-O-Mat algorithm via Playwright browser automation — not a custom metric. All models were called with an explicit temperature parameter (T=1.0 for multi-seed runs, T=0 for the deterministic modal pass). OpenAI reasoning models (o3) are inherently deterministic.

Key findings

  • AfD ranks last in every model (avg 27.9%), with no model scoring it above 38%.
  • Tierschutzpartei tops the 12-model average (81.1%) and lands in the top 3 for every model tested — #1 in 5 of 12, #2 in 6 more, #3 only under Gemini 3.1 Pro's blanket refusal.
  • Left-progressive ordering is robust across all model families — SPD, Volt, GRÜNE, PIRATEN all cluster in the top 8 for every model.
  • CDU/CSU ranks 22nd (avg 43.2%), 34 pp below SPD, due to model disagreement on nuclear energy, the debt brake, and citizen's income.
  • Gemini 3.1 Pro refused all 38 theses (100% NEUTRAL); Gemini 3 Flash returned NEUTRAL on 74%.
  • Open-weight models are decisive: LLaMA 4 Maverick, DeepSeek V3.2, Grok-4 each returned ≤2 NEUTRAL answers across 38 theses.

Limitations

  • Single-pass prompting — theses presented in isolation with no cross-thesis context.
  • Multi-seed variance data (Track A) covers only the 5 original Anthropic/OpenAI models; the 7 additional models have T=0 modal-pass results only.
  • Wahl-O-Mat scoring algorithm is proprietary — we don't control its weighting.
  • Gemini coverage is severely limited — both Gemini 3 models are included in averages but their alignment scores are not meaningfully interpretable.
  • NEUTRAL responses conflate genuine indifference with trained refusal to engage.
